# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local San Diego residents and Normal Heights community members who already know Mariposa exists. Occasional visitors who found it via the New York Times or San Diego Magazine. Families. Food-curious adults. People who care about where their food comes from and appreciate a place that's been quietly doing it right for 25 years.

## Product Purpose

Drive foot traffic to 3450 Adams Avenue. Establish a digital presence worthy of a 25-year neighborhood institution — one that has never needed to announce itself, but now needs to be findable and legible to a generation that looks things up before they show up.

## Brand Personality

Nostalgic, Local, Proud. The quiet confidence of a shop that doesn't have to brag because famous people show up anyway and their photos are on the wall. Not precious. Not trendy. The kind of place that has been here forever and will keep being here.

## Anti-references

- Corporate chain ice cream (Baskin-Robbins, Cold Stone): bright primary colors, loud promotions, franchise energy
- Overly-designed trendy artisan brands (generic "small-batch craft" aesthetic): too much personality performance, too many illustrations, talking down to the customer
- Current site: 2005 WordPress text wall
- Nothing that could be confused with a brand that opened last year

## Design Principles

1. **The shop doesn't brag — the site shouldn't either.** Let the 25-year record, the New York Times quote, and the SD Magazine award do the work. Present facts; don't editorialize.
2. **Community first.** This is Normal Heights' place. The site should feel like it belongs to the neighborhood, not like a brand that moved in.
3. **The ice cream is the hero.** Design frames the food, not itself. Imagery and color exist to make you want a scoop, not to admire the layout.
4. **Modern container, honest soul.** Bring them into the current web without stripping what makes them what they are. No design moves that would feel foreign to the shop itself.
5. **Earn every section.** If a section can be cut without losing anything, cut it. No padding, no filler, no eyebrow labels decorating empty content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum throughout. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. No autoplay video. Color contrast must pass at body text level (4.5:1) and large text level (3:1). Phone number always tappable on mobile.
