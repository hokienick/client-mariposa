# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Normal Heights families, locals on Adams Avenue, food-curious San Diegans who look things up before they show up. Occasional visitors who found Mariposa via the New York Times or San Diego Magazine. People who care about where their food comes from.

## Product Purpose

Drive foot traffic to 3450 Adams Avenue. Be findable and legible — a digital presence worthy of a 26-year neighborhood institution that has never needed to announce itself, but now needs to be discoverable to people who look things up before they show up.

## Brand Personality

Nostalgic, Welcoming, Permanent. The quiet pride of a place that has been here forever and will keep being here. Not precious. Not trendy. An old-school ice cream parlor on a sunny San Diego street — bright, warm, family-friendly.

## Anti-references

- Corporate chain ice cream (Baskin-Robbins, Cold Stone): bright primary colors, franchise energy, loud promotions
- Overly-designed artisan brands: too much personality performance, illustration-heavy, "small-batch craft" posturing
- Current site: 2005 WordPress text wall
- Anything that looks like it opened last year
- Dark/moody aesthetic: this is a bright, warm, family-friendly neighborhood place

## Design Principles

1. **Light, warm, welcoming.** This is a family ice cream parlor on a sunny San Diego street. Bright, inviting, no drama.
2. **The shop doesn't brag — the site shouldn't either.** Let the 26-year record, the New York Times quote, and the SD Magazine award do the work. Present facts, don't editorialize.
3. **The ice cream is the hero.** Design frames the food. Photography exists to make you want a scoop, not to admire the layout.
4. **Community first.** This is Normal Heights' place. The site should feel like it belongs to the neighborhood.
5. **Earn every section.** No padding, no filler, no decoration for decoration's sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA throughout. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Phone number always tappable on mobile. Color contrast ≥4.5:1 for body text.
